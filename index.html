Our Mission

We provide simple, easy to replicate homemade recipes (my mother's recipes) for even the novice in cooking. Our recipes are especially useful for college students who tend to put on the "Freshman 15" due to eating fast food constantly throughout the semester due to no home-cooked meals from their family. Too often recipe books and websites provide either entirely too much information, or not enough information for their recipes. Another problem with other recipe websites, is that they do no take into account that many people are on a budget (low income families, college students, etc.) and their ingredients list does not reflect that. The ingredients used on Belinda's Cookbook will have the price for each ingredient listed.
